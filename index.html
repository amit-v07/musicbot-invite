üìö Frequently Asked Questions

Is this Music Bot really free?

Yes! Our Discord Music Bot is 100% free to use with no premium paywalls for high-quality audio or essential features like autoplay.

How do I play music from Spotify?

Simply use the command !play <spotify link>. The bot supports tracks, albums, and playlists from Spotify directly.

Why is the bot audio lagging?

We host on high-performance servers to minimize lag. If you experience issues, try changing your Discord server region or check your internet connection.
